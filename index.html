    An in-progress decompilation of Luigi's Mansion for the Nintendo GameCube.
    The focus is currently laid on the Japanese release (GLMJ01), but configurations exist for all available versions.
    The original creator first focused on the North American release (GLME01), and it's symbol map was put in a backup file.

    There are no known symbol maps for this game, however, it does come with RTTI.
